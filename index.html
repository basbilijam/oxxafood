About Oxxafood

Oxxafood is a wholesaler of organic fruit and vegetables based in Veenendaal (Netherlands) and Loriol (France). We deliver products in a contemporary and modern way, from our suppliers to professional customers throughout Europe and beyond.

Oxxafood is the link between the daily demand from professional customers for organic products and the supply of many producers, and organizes for both parties the sales, purchasing and logistics.

What we do

Oxxafood has set itself the goal of rendering organic fruit and vegetables more easily accessible for professional market clients. Due to a strong increase in consumption, and the comparably slow increase in terms of production, there is a need for more information, overview and clarity in this young and rapidly growing market sector.

Oxxafood has a large network in the organic fruit and vegetables sector, both nationally and internationally, and makes relevant information accessible to buyers and providers of organic fruit and vegetables.
